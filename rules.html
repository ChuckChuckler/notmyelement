Rules

You are the doorman of the PT Apartment Complex, tasked with one simple task-- let the elements who live there inside.

BUT BE WARNED...

...for there are imposters attempting to disguise themselves.

An element will come at the door, announcing its identity.

You will receive a paper with their validation info, such as:

  • PT family
  • Energy level
  • (Shorthand) electron configuration

(Among others...)

The imposters will try this, too-- but they are somewhat clueless.

Search for mistakes within the validation info to weed out the doppelganger elements from the real ones.

Use the periodic table given to you on your right wall to verify the information on the element's ID.

If you think you've found a fake element, click the button! But be careful-- calling imposter on innocent elements may get you fired...!

Good luck, and whatever you do...

...DON'T LET THE DOPPELGANGERS IN!!!
